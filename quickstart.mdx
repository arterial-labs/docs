## Initial Setup

Jump into your own account and organization

<AccordionGroup>
	<Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Create your account">
		Sign up for Arterial at [arterial.design](https://arterial.design) and
		complete the onboarding process. You'll get access to the dashboard where
		you can create your first project and explore the platform's capabilities.
	</Accordion>
	<Accordion icon="folder-plus" title="Create your first project">
		Navigate to the [Dashboard](https://arterial.design/app/overview) and click
		"New Project" to get started. Choose your project location and basic
		parameters to begin exploring parcels and development opportunities in your
		area.
	</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Core Workflow

Begin analyzing more than 8 million parcels across North America using Arterial's policy and dataset engines.

<AccordionGroup>
	<Accordion icon="search" title="Search and analyze parcels">
		Use Arterial's parcel search functionality to find development
		opportunities. Filter by zoning, size, ownership, and other criteria to
		identify the best parcels for your projects. Learn more about [parcel search
		techniques](/workflows/parcel-search).
	</Accordion>
	<Accordion icon="cuboid" title="Generate massing studies">
		Once you've identified a parcel, use the massing tools to explore different
		building configurations. Test various heights, setbacks, and building forms
		to understand what's possible on your site. See our [massing
		guide](/essentials/massing) for details.
	</Accordion>
	<Accordion icon="layout-grid" title="Design your layout">
		Move from massing to detailed layout planning. Arrange units, common areas,
		and circulation to optimize your development. Check out [layout
		essentials](/essentials/layout) to get started.
	</Accordion>
	<Accordion icon="calculator" title="Run financial analysis">
		Use Arterial's built-in financial modeling to understand project viability.
		Calculate costs, revenues, and returns to make informed development
		decisions. Learn more in our [finances section](/essentials/finances).
	</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
